The need for meshnet arises from the fact that how we connect and form the network around us today. Let us try to understand in simpler terms.

How do we connect today?

To get connected to Internet, today we go and approach an organization called Internet Service Provider (ISP). In India examples of ISPs are,

  1. BSNL
  2. Airtel
  3. ACT Broadband
  4. List of more ISPs in India can be found here

The ISPs gives us data plans to choose and once we confirm the plan, they will extend a wire (or) cable (if it is a wired broadband connection) or they will setup a wireless transceiver at the top of the building (if it is a wireless connection). Different ISPs have inter-connection between each other, so even if you have a connection from BSNL and if your friend have a connection from Airtel, you both can still communicate.
